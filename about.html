Achieve high quality, contemporaneous bedside diagnosis.

Grupo Giliapos has produced a new digital product App-Based Click-a-Diagnosis (ABCD) that aims to achieve high-quality, contemporaneous bedside diagnosis while remaining user-friendly.

It has an easy-to-use click-based format that encourages the user to move from presumptive diagnosis to the likely underlying cause of clinical problems.

By using a web-based application, thereby removing the need to download an extra app onto the phone or user interface of the end-user the risk of cluttering your screen is avoided.

our story

The Problem

In a variety of clinical situations, one of the problems clinicians face is the ability to access a resource that is simple to use and easy to access. In resource-poor countries, there is the additional effect of manpower challenges and brain drain, meaning the available clinical workforce is overstretched. Consequently, there is a need for bedside efficiency in arriving at diagnoses or unraveling clinical problems.

our story

The Solution

We have produced a clinical aid which we call App-Based Click-a-Diagnosis (ABCD). This is an easy-to-use, accessible, evidence-based, quality resource that enables rapid bedside diagnosis for clinicians wherever they but is especially useful in areas of the world with manpower challenges. It can also be used as an initial triage tool that enables first-line responders to speak with more senior clinicians to optimize the management of patients while ensuring remote supervision.

By following our simple registration steps, you have easy access to this high-quality resource and we have made it available at no cost to you the user for a one-month trial.
